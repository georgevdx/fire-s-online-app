Fire-S RC 1.1.20D - Module 04 Render Queue

Purpose:
- Add a central render queue layer without replacing the legacy app.js flow.
- Coalesce repeated dashboard/premises renders into a single animation frame.
- Block Home / Executive Snapshot render calls while Premises or Inspection screens are active.
- Reduce visual bounce and ghost-dashboard flashes caused by overlapping render calls.

Files changed:
- app.js: version updated only.
- index.html: cache-busting updated and modules/render-queue.js loaded after state-manager.js.
- modules/render-queue.js: new module.
