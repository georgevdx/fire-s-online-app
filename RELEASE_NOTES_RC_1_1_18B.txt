Fire-S RC 1.1.18B - Projects/Premises Layout Stability Patch

Purpose:
- Reduce the remaining screen "hop" on the Projects/Premises page.

- Passive background sync is skipped while the Projects/Premises list is open.
- Manual sync/refresh workflows remain available.
- Projects/Premises cards now have stable minimum heights.
- Hover/focus transform movement is disabled on list cards.

- Card colour/status changes are limited to visual-only transitions.

Test focus:
- Open Projects and leave it idle for more than 20 seconds.
