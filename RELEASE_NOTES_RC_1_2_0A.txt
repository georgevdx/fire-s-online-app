Fire-S RC 1.2.0A - Inspection Lifecycle UX

- Projects button in inspection flow changed to Close / Close Inspection.
- Close inspection now offers Save & Close, Discard & Close, or Continue Inspection.
- Simplified Projects page into lifecycle filters: Scheduled, New Premise, Continue Current, Previous Cycles, Archive / New Cycle.
- New or unassessed premises no longer show as high risk. They show as Not Assessed or Scheduled - Not Started.
- Card primary actions are clearer: Start First Inspection, Continue / Edit Current, View Previous Cycles.
