Fire-S Sprint 111.0 - Dedicated Analytics Tab

Purpose:
Moves statistical, trend, category and AI-ready management information out of ordinary inspection pages and into a separate Analytics workspace.

Added:
- Bottom action bar: Analytics button
- Inspection menu: Analytics button
- Hidden Analytics card shown only when opened
- Analytics tiles: Compliance, Building Health, Risk, Open Actions
- Action Summary by priority
- Category Performance
- Priority Action Categories
- Top 5 Priority Action Items
- AI-ready Summary

Design principle:
Operational pages remain clean for field inspection work. Management and statistical data lives under Analytics.
