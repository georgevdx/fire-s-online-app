Fire-S RC 1.0.5 - Photo Categories + Scroll Stability

Added:
- Photo category dropdown for every captured photo.
- Default photo category: General Evidence.
- Categories include Fire Equipment, Sprinklers, Emergency Lighting, Escape Routes, Hazardous Substances, etc.
- Photo category is saved with the inspection photo metadata.
- Photo category appears in PDF photo appendix metadata.
- Compact backup photo metadata now keeps the category.

Improved:
- Checklist navigation scrolls only when the active question is outside the comfortable viewport.
- This reduces jumping back to the top during question navigation.

Notes:
- Conditional sections and full analytics dashboard are not included in this patch yet.
- This patch is intended as a safe incremental RC update.
